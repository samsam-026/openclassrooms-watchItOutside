Who are we?


We are Watch It Outside Events Co. here to share with you our love of filmography. This time, we're bringing this love all the way to Boston.

From the 5-8th of August 2020, we will be showcasing 8 must-watch classic movies. The showcased movies are appropriate for people of all ages to enjoy.

Join us from 6pm till midnight at the Boston Public Garden on the above dates, for an evening of fun. Make sure to reserve a seat, so we can hold a spot for you and your friends.
